Q
What are GongZi.XiaoBai's features?
A GongZi.XiaoBai's currently supported features include: Chatting, Setting Alarms and Reminders, Information Searching/Gathering, Checking the Weather, playing Music, News, Stories. It also has 10 expressions along with the ability to interact naturally with humans through natural language processing and analysis. It can also control home appliances and devices (currently supports air conditioners only).
Q
How do I control GongZi.XiaoBai?
A You can control GongZi.XiaoBai solely through voice commands. It utilises its high-precision voice recognition software to accurately deduce you commands and executes them. Alternatively, you can control it through the mobile applications.
Q
What differentiates GongZi.XiaoBai from the other similar products in the market?
A The 'robots' that are available on the market nowadays are aimed towards children as toys and are usually branded on popular media. GongZi.XiaoBai is an highly intelligent robot enabled through advanced artificial intelligence technologies. Apart from being able to have natural interactions with you, it's defining feature is it's ability to self-learn new words, giving it a dynamic knowledge system. It can also understand you habits over time to become a pet robot with intelligence just like an infant; young and capable to absorb new knowledge and learn!
Q
What are the enabled COUPLE features?
A Using the mobile app, you can pair two GongZi.XiaoBais together to form a relationship between them. You can then sync reminders between them or greet both of them over a distance. As for more 'intimate' interactions between the robots, it shall be left up to your creativity to explore them!
Q
Can GongZi.XiaoBai move or dance?
A GongZi.XiaoBai is designed to be a smart-home companion and do not have movement capabilities as of now.
Q
Does GongZi.XiaoBai have expressions or head movements?
A Yes, GongZi.XiaoBai is able to rotate its head up to 180° to face you whenever you speak to it. It also has the ability to express its emotions through 10 expressions, for example, happiness, sadness, etc.
